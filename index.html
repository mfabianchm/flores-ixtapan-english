Â¿Who we are?

Flores Ixtapan is the most experienced company in Mexico. We started in 1979 with production of roses under greenhouses in Mexico in Villa Guerrero, Ixtapan de la Sal, Tenancingo and Zumpahuacan, in the State of Mexico. These places have the necessary microclimates to produce the very high quality required by consumers within and outside the country.

Production

We have 80 hectares of production of flowers in the south of State of Mexico. The company was the pioneer in the production of flowers under greenhouses. The success of entrepreneurs in the region has achieved the production of thousands of hectares of flowers under greenhouses.

Our clients

Nowadays we are suppliers of the main supermarkets in Mexico.

Additionally we export our different species of flowers and a diverse number of products to different regions of the USA and south of Canada.
